# Monetization Playbook

This project should not be positioned as a generic AI script generator. The first monetizable offer is a lightweight short-video content service for local businesses and creators.

## Day-1 Revenue Goal

Target: earn the first `¥20` by selling one paid sample run.

Offer:

- Customer sends one photo, video, or rough idea.
- You deliver 3 short-video topic angles.
- You deliver 1 shootable script.
- You deliver 5 hook titles.
- Price: `¥20` for the first run.

This is intentionally small. The goal is not high margin on the first order; the goal is to prove someone will pay for the workflow.

## Best First Customers

Start with people who already need short videos but do not have a content team:

- Restaurants, cafes, dessert shops, bars
- Beauty salons, nail shops, hair salons
- Gyms, yoga studios, personal trainers
- Homestays, hotels, local attractions
- Small product sellers and live-commerce creators
- Creators doing Xiaohongshu, Douyin, TikTok, Reels, or Shorts

## Sales Message

Chinese:

```text
我现在在做一个短视频内容试跑服务。
你发我一张图、一段视频，或者一段想法，我收 20 元帮你出一版：
1. 3 个适合你店/账号的短视频选题
2. 1 条可以直接拍的脚本
3. 5 个标题/开头钩子
如果这次有用，后面可以给你做每周内容包。
```

English:

```text
I am testing a short-video content service for local businesses.
For $3, send me one photo, video, or raw idea and I will return:
1. 3 shootable short-video topic angles
2. 1 practical filming script
3. 5 hook titles you can post with
If the first run is useful, I can turn it into a weekly content pack.
```

## Delivery Process

1. Ask the customer for one piece of material and their platform.
2. Paste the material description into the web app.
3. Complete the guided questions based on the customer context.
4. Generate topics.
5. Pick the strongest topic and generate the script.
6. Send the customer a clean message with:
   - 3 topic titles
   - 1 selected script
   - 5 hooks
   - 1 filming note specific to their business

## Upsell Path

After the first paid run, offer a simple monthly package:

- `¥199/month`: 20 topics + 8 scripts per month
- `¥499/month`: 60 topics + 20 scripts + weekly content direction
- `¥999/month`: team/agency package for multiple accounts

The first upsell should be simple: "I can do this every week for you."

## Validation Metrics

Track these manually at first:

- Number of people contacted
- Number of replies
- Number of paid sample runs
- Number of customers who ask for a second run
- Number of customers who upgrade to a monthly package

If fewer than 1 in 20 contacted prospects pays for the sample, revise the offer or choose a narrower customer group.
